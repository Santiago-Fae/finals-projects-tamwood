Alaska

This was the state of Alaska before she was rescued and given a beautiful family.
She had many fleas and injuries.
She was also malnourished and extremely thin.
She was very strong to survive... But look how she is now:

After

The happy ending:

Todays Alaska is a happy and healthy dog.
Actually, she is 4 months.

Some photos

Alaska is an amazing dog, full of energy and love. She has always been kind, playful, and loyal, making everyone around her happy. Now, she is doing very well, healthy and strong. She enjoys running, playing, and receiving affection from those who love her. Alaska’s bright eyes and wagging tail show how happy she is. She is a truly special dog, bringing joy and warmth to every moment.
