Projeto Adota Pet

Adota Pet is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to the protection, rescue, and responsible adoption of abandoned or vulnerable animals. Founded with the aim of promoting animal welfare and raising awareness about the importance of adoption, Adota Pet acts as a link between animals in need of a home and people willing to offer love, care, and a safe environment.

About

Adota Pet's mission is to reduce the number of abandoned animals on the streets, combating abandonment and mistreatment. The NGO believes that all animals deserve dignity, respect, and a happy life.
